App description

Our website is dedicated to helping students select the right college, so I, our main users are students who are about to graduate from high school, in addition to students' parents or teachers who may also visit our website to help students choose their future colleges. Of course, anyone who wants to learn about colleges may visit our site.The site will display a ranked list representing each university based on the user's choices. Each row of the list will match the user's preferences, and when the user clicks on one of the universities, basic information about that university will be displayed. We currently base this site on the data. We will also be looking for more data to support our site afterwards.

With our web app, users will be able to find universities or colleges that best fit their interests or other information gathered in the survey. Before suggesting results to users, we will first interact by having them take a quick survey to ask about their preferred academic interests, SAT/ACT scores, tuition rates, or any other criteria they want to use to look for their universities. Also, once users decide on some universities that they are interested in, we will provide a feature that allows them to easily compare their favorite universities to help them make a quick decision. We will quickly be able to collect users' information about their preferences based on the survey. Then, we will try to reduce users' work and find universities for them that best match their interests. Therefore, users will be able to save time in deciding on universities and focus on preparing for other admissions to the university.
